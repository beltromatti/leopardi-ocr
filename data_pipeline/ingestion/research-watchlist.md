## High-Interest Watchlist

Why it matters:

- likely useful for understanding current synthetic document parsing practice

Current policy:

- research only until schema and license review is complete

### CHURRO-DS and related recent historical-text releases

Why it matters:

- stronger modern historical-handwriting coverage

Current policy:

- research only until access and target compatibility are verified

- `D4LA`

- `UniMER-1M`

Examples:

Current policy:

- allowed for triage, disagreement mining, and weak auxiliary pools
